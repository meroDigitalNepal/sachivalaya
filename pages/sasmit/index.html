About

Sasmit Pokharel is the Minister for Education, Science and Technology, representing Kathmandu District No. 5 with the Rastriya Swatantra Party (RSP). Born, raised, and educated in Kathmandu, he holds a Master's in Public Administration and Anti-Corruption from Tribhuvan University, along with a BBM and an LLB from Kathmandu University.

Focus Areas

Good governance and digital transformation, youth employment, and structural reform aligned with citizen movements.
